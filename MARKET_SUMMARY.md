# SupplyVault - Market Summary for Feasibility Analysis

## Executive Overview

**SupplyVault** is an AI-powered compliance management platform designed specifically for the textile supply chain industry. It centralizes certification management, automates data entry, and provides real-time compliance monitoring for suppliers and brands.

---

## Purpose

### The Problem We Solve
The textile industry faces significant challenges in managing supplier compliance:
- **Manual tracking** of certifications leads to errors and lost documents
- **Scattered information** across emails, spreadsheets, and file systems
- **Reactive approach** to expiry management results in compliance lapses
- **Zero visibility** for brands into their supplier networks
- **Time-consuming processes** with hours spent on manual data entry

### Our Solution
SupplyVault transforms compliance management from a manual, reactive process into an automated, proactive system that saves time, reduces risk, and provides complete supply chain visibility.

---

## Core Features

### 1. Centralized Certification Management
- **Single platform** for all supplier certifications (GOTS, OEKO-TEX, SA8000, etc.)
- **Cloud-based storage** with secure document access
- **Organized tracking** eliminating lost or scattered documents
- **Complete audit trail** of all certification activities

### 2. AI-Powered Automation
- **Automatic data extraction** from PDF certificates using Claude AI
- **90% time savings** on data entry (5 minutes → 30 seconds)
- **Intelligent form auto-fill** with confidence scoring
- **Error reduction** through automated processing

### 3. Proactive Expiry Management
- **Real-time alerts** for upcoming certificate expiries
- **Color-coded urgency** indicators (7-day, 30-day, 90-day windows)
- **Visual timeline** of expiring certifications
- **Email notifications** to prevent compliance lapses

### 4. Analytics & Reporting
- **Interactive dashboards** for compliance overview
- **Trend analysis** of certification activity over time
- **Compliance scoring** (0-100 scale)
- **Distribution charts** by certification type
- **Exportable reports** for audits and stakeholders

### 5. Network Effect
- **Supplier profiles** with certification portfolios
- **Brand connections** for direct supplier monitoring
- **Network-wide visibility** for brands across all suppliers
- **Verification status** tracking

### 6. Document Management
- **Secure cloud storage** (AWS S3 integration)
- **Document viewer** for PDFs and images
- **Download & print** capabilities
- **Permission-based access** control
